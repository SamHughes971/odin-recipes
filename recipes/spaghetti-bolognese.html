Spaghetti Bolognese Recipe

Summary

Fancy a delicious, yet simple Italian classic? Try our spaghetti bolognese recipe! This timeless classic will satisfy anyone's tastebuds.

Timings and Servings

  • Prep Time: 25 mins
  • Cook Time: 1 hour and 15 mins
  • Servings: 6

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp of olive oil
  • 4 rashers smoked streaky bacon
  • 2 medium onions
  • 2 carrots
  • 2 celery sticks
  • 500g beef mince
  • 2 x 400g tins of plum tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp of tomato purée
  • 1 beef stock cube
  • 125ml red wine (it does not need to be expensive)
  • 2 fresh bay leaves
  • 400g spaghetti

Directions

  1. Put a saucepan on medium heat and add 1 tbsp of olive oil.
  2. Chop the 4 bacon rashers finely. Fry them until golden and crispy.
  3. Reduce the heat and add the 2 celery sticks, 2 medium onions and 2 carrots. Stir the vegetables regularly until they soften.
  4. Increase the heat to medium-high and add 500g of beef mince. Keep constantly stirring the beef mince until it starts to brown.
  5. Add the two tins of plum tomatoes, 2 bay leaves, 1 beef stock cube, 2 tbsp of tomato purée, and 125 ml of red wine. Combine the ingredients thoroughly until the plum tomatoes are broken up.
  6. Bring it to a gentle simmer and cover with a lid. Cook for 1 hour and 15 minutes. Stir occasionally.
  7. Once the bolognese is nearly cooked, prepare the 400g of spaghetti ( follow the instructions on the package). Drain the spaghetti and add it to the bolognese.
  8. Enjoy!
