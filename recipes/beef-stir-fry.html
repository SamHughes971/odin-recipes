Beef Stir-Fry

Summary

Are you short on time, or have some leftover ingredients in the fridge? Look no further than our simple, but delicious beef stir-fry!

Timings and Servings

  • Prep Time: 15 mins
  • Cook Time: 10 mins
  • Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • 1 pound of beef sirloin
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • 2 carrots
  • 1 green onion
  • 1 teaspoon of minced garlic
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons sesame seeds
  • 1 cup of broccoli florets

Directions

  1. Put your vegetable oil in a large wok or skillet over medium-high.
  2. Start by adding your beef sirloin into your wok or skillet until the beef starts to brown.
  3. Move the beef to one side and add your broccoli, bell pepper, green onion, carrots, and garlic in the center of the wok or skillet. Cook for approximately 2 minutes.
  4. Mix your beef and all the ingredients you added in step 3 together. Finally, add your soy sauce and sesame seeds. Continue to stir until the vegetables become tender.
  5. Enjoy!
